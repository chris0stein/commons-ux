New Member Introduction

Congratulations and thank you for completing the Registration process for the CUNY Academic Commons. You have joined a strong, diverse community of CUNYItes representing all campuses and the Central Office, faculty, staff, administrators and students.

The Commons also hosts a diverse feature set. You can start a blog, join or create a group, write and get feedback on a paper, connect with a colleague and more.

In order to help you get started with your experience on the Commons we've provided links to articles, videos and major features.
